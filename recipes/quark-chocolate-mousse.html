Quark Chocolate Mousse

Description

A description.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 250g fat-free quark
  • 5g cacao powder (or cocoa powder)
  • 10g Options Belgian Hot Chocolate Powder
  • 1g Vanilla Extract
  • 2tsp sweetener
  • 2 Fiber One bars
  • 50g sliced strawberries
  • halo top ice cream

Steps

  1. Add the quark to a jug, along with the cacao powder, chocolate powder, sweetener and vanilla extract.
  2. Whisk the ingredients by hand, or with an electric beater, until combined.
  3. Place Fiber One bars in small containers (eg ramekins or small glasses/bowls).
  4. Pour the quark mousse mixture on top and put the ramekins in the fridge for a couple of hours.
  5. Remove from the fridge and serve with chopped strawberries and ice cream.
